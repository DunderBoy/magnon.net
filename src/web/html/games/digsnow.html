DIG SNOW!

In Dig Snow! your goal is to dig as many piles of snow as you can before the time runs out. Can you get the highest score?

Dig Snow! was made for Ludum Dare 31 which is a game creator challenge we participated in on December 5th-8th, 2014. The game is simple and easy to learn. The people who have played the game has said that it is quite addicting and fun to play together with friends in the competition to reach the highest score.
